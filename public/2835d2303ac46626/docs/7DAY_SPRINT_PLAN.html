7-Day Sprint Plan — Stage 1 (Versley) ~1 Week Out

You have ~7 days. This compresses the 10-day plan and front-loads Versley's home turf (pretraining, domain adaptation, VLMs, classical NLP) and your paper defense, with the new adaptive-simulation findings (docs 18, 18b, 19) folded in. Assumes ~3–4 focused hours/day. Read the model answers out loud, not silently.

The one rule that beats everything else (from 16 simulations): stop inventing numbers. Your single highest-risk failure mode was stating benchmark scores, paper IDs, and infra stats you can't source. If you can't cite it exactly, say "I don't have that figure in front of me" and give the directional claim. And self-flag uncertainty in your first sentence, not after the follow-up lands — this happened in 12 of 16 sessions.
