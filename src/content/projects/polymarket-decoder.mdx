A self-driven side project. Polymarket aggregates beliefs from people putting real money behind outcomes; the order book is a minute-by-minute record of what the crowd actually believes. I'm collecting traces around scheduled events (elections, rulings, rate decisions) to study what moves prices and when crowds get it wrong. Repo coming soon.
