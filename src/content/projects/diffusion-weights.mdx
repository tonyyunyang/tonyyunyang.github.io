Explored whether neural network fine-tuning can be reframed as a generative parameter-prediction problem instead of step-by-step SGD. A U-Net diffusion model forecasts future weight states from early checkpoints; predictions across short, medium, and long horizons are merged into the final model. On MMLU, OpenBookQA, and WMT16 the method delivered 3.2× end-to-end speedup with downstream performance comparable to full fine-tuning.
